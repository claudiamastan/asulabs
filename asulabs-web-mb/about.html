Mission

Our aim is to improve overall health and wellness through herbs, supplements, and natural remedies that have been used for centuries dating back to the most ancient civilizations. What Does “Asu” Mean?

In ancient Mesopotamia, ‘asu’ were doctors who specialized in herbal remedies and dealt with empirical applications of medication. Our name reflects our commitment to sourcing natural ingredients.
